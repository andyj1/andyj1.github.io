CVPR 2026 Itinerary

The Mile High City • Denver, Colorado

🏨 Basecamp: Staybridge Suites Denver Downtown

Address: 333 W Colfax Ave. (Just blocks from the Convention Center!)

Right Outside Your Door:
  • Civic Center Park: A massive green space connecting the downtown area to the Capitol. Great for a morning stretch.
  • Denver Mint: Literally around the corner. (Requires advanced online booking for free tours).
  • Denver Art Museum: Only a 10-minute walk south.
  • Coffee: Vibe Coffee and Wine is highly walkable for a quick morning brew or evening wind-down.

🚆 Transit Guide for First-Timers

A-Line (Airport to Union Station):
  • Buy a ticket at the vending machines before boarding ($10.50 Airport Day Pass). Valid for unlimited rides that day.
  • Trains run every 15 minutes during the day. The ride to downtown takes 37 minutes.
Free 16th Street MallRide:
  • Hop-on, hop-off free shuttle that runs the length of 16th Street. No ticket required.
  • Stops at every block. Great for getting from Union Station toward the State Capitol.
RTD Light Rail (General):
  • Buy a ticket at a TVM on the platform. Credit cards accepted.
  • Important: If you use a physical ticket, you MUST validate it at the red ticket validator machine on the platform before boarding. Valid for 3 hours.
